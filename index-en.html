The Firm

Public Law and Corporate practice since 1997

Based in Brasília, the firm focuses on complex legal matters that require careful analysis, institutional understanding and a strategic approach.

Founded in 1997, the firm has developed a practice focused on solving issues involving Public Law, state-owned enterprises, public contracts, strategic civil litigation, Corporate Law, M&A and governance.

The work combines technical rigour, a systemic reading of the legal framework and constant dialogue with court precedents, especially those of Brazil’s Superior Courts and Audit Courts, while remaining attentive to the institutional and business reality of clients.

The firm maintains a calm, discreet and objective posture, with clear and realistic communication on risks, alternatives and available legal strategies, always in strict compliance with Brazilian Bar rules.

About the partner

The firm is led by Edval Freire Jr. Caiado, a corporate lawyer and legal opinion writer (parecerista), practising since 1997 in Public and Private Law. Over the years, he has built experience in complex contracts, governance, regulatory matters, strategic litigation and advisory work for companies and institutions.

With an interdisciplinary background, he brings together legal technique and an understanding of economic and management dynamics in both the public and private sectors, providing consistent analysis grounded in precedents, respected scholarship and sound governance practices.
